Top attractions in Kashmir:

Dal Lake (Srinagar)

Dal Lake is the heart of Srinagar, known for its iconic shikara (boat) rides and floating houseboats. The lake is surrounded by lush gardens, snow-capped mountains, and traditional Kashmiri culture.

Gulmarg

Pahalgam

Known for its scenic beauty and serene environment, Pahalgam offers picturesque views of lush valleys, rivers, and meadows.

Sonamarg

Sonamarg, also known as the "Meadow of Gold," is a tranquil town surrounded by snow-covered peaks, glaciers, and pristine rivers.

How to Reach

  • By Air: Srinagar Airport (15 km from city center).
  • By Train: Jammu Tawi is the nearest railway station.
  • By Road: Well-connected through National Highways.

Trending Activities

Tulip Festival

Held annually in Srinagar, the Tulip Festival showcases the vibrant tulip gardens in full bloom.

Stay in a Houseboat

Spend a night in a traditional Kashmiri houseboat on Dal Lake or Nigeen Lake. These houseboats are beautifully crafted with intricate woodwork and offer a one-of-a-kind experience.

Skiing and Snow Sports in Gulmarg

In winter, Gulmarg transforms into a skiing paradise, offering world-class facilities for skiing, snowboarding, and other snow sports. It is often referred to as the "Mecca of Indian Skiing."
